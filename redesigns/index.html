Concept 01

The editorial — a fortnightly journal of letters from the road.

Reads like a thoughtful travel magazine. Warm cream paper, big serif headlines, an "Issue Nº" masthead and a cover-story layout. Plays to your strength as a writer — the long-form essays are the hero, not a side note.

The travel journal — polaroids, postcards, a passport full of stamps.

Playful and warm. A paper-textured background, handwritten titles, polaroids fixed with washi tape, a dashed-line route map across South-east Asia and a postcard-shaped newsletter sign-up. The most family-feeling, the most fun, the most "us".

The minimalist — clean, modern, mostly whitespace.

Quiet and confident. A bone-white page, oversized clean type with italic-serif accents, a "currently filing from" live indicator, and stories listed as a clean numbered index. Lets the photography do the work. Feels like a contemporary design studio's site.
