Note · 02 / About

Why east?

We are J, R, E and e — one family from the UK. We recognised the limits that mainstream schooling has for our two neurodiverse boys, sold most of what we owned, and booked one-way tickets from Heathrow to Bangkok.

The West's bargain — work until you retire, then live the life you wanted — assumes a perfect health and a surviving pension we are not prepared to bet our family on. So we are showing the boys, rather than telling them, that there is more than one way to live.
